⭐ WHAT YOU CAN SHOW (100% REAL, FROM META APIs)
1. Story Reach (Impressions)

From the merchant’s business account — NOT from the shopper’s account.

When a user tags the merchant, that mention comes into the merchant’s Media Insights.

You get:

reach

impressions

taps forward/back

exits

This is big enough to prove “This campaign generated X total reach.”

✓ Totally real
✓ Merchants will love it
✓ No permission issues

2. Total Verified Posts

Count of how many Stories were actually posted and verified.

You can show:

total Stories posted

completion rate

missed-post rate

average discount vs completion

Basic but valuable.

3. Aggregated Reach Per Day / Week / Month

Just sum all impressions across all verified Stories.

This becomes your core graph.

4. Savings vs Exposure

Brands love this formula:

“You gave away £1,200 in discounts and got 184,000 impressions back.”

It’s ROI porn.

5. Average Creator Size

Because you store follower count at checkout.

Show:

average follower count

distribution (histogram)

top-tier creators

Easy to generate, nice to display.

6. Top Performing Customer Posts

You cannot show the story media itself (privacy),
BUT you can show:

IG username

reach/impressions

timestamp

Looks professional without breaking rules.

7. Followers Gained by the Merchant

This one is very possible.

You have merchant’s IG Business Account ID, so you can track:

followers count each day

increases during Spiral activity

correlation with Story posting volume

This becomes a sexy graph.

8. New Customers → Returning via Spiral

Since you store Spiral user IDs, you can show:

repeat Spiral shoppers

avg spend from Spiral users

lifetime value vs non-Spiral customers

Merchants eat that up.

⭐ WHAT YOU CAN SHOW BUT WITH CONDITIONS

These are possible but require careful compliance.

9. Clicks on Merchant’s Profile (from the Story mention)

Instagram Business Insights gives you:

profile visits from tagged mentions

So you can show:

“Spiral Stories drove 312 profile visits.”

Merchants will cry with happiness.

10. Website taps from tagged Stories

You get:

“website clicks” on merchant profile
NOT from the shopper’s story directly.

Still very valuable.
